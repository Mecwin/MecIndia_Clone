Mecwin Technologies

Mecwin Technologies, a leading Indian manufacturer, specializes in advanced solar pumps, MPPT controllers, PMSM motors, fluid pumps, EV motors, and special-purpose motors for medical, defense, and aerospace applications. With a commitment to sustainability and innovation, Mecwin delivers reliable solutions across renewable energy, agriculture, automation, and industrial sectors, driving efficiency and environmental responsibility.

Vision

Mecwin's journey began in 2017 with a modest 80 sq. ft. facility and a vision to innovate for a greener world. Over the years, the company has expanded its manufacturing capabilities, growing its footprint and workforce to meet the demands of diverse industries. Mecwin’s dedication to technological advancement and sustainability has positioned it as a key player in India’s industrial landscape.

Mission

At Mecwin Technologies, our mission is to lead the way in transforming our world into a greener, more sustainable place. We are dedicated to innovating and delivering solutions that not only meet the needs of today but also preserve the planet for future generations.
