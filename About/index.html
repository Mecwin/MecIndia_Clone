Bonfiglioli India 

Bonfiglioli, a global leader in power transmission and drive solutions, has firmly established itself as a pivotal player in the Indian industrial landscape. With a rich history spanning over two decades, a robust manufacturing footprint, and an extensive product portfolio catering to diverse applications, Bonfiglioli India has consistently driven innovation and contributed significantly to the nation's industrial growth.

A Legacy of Presence and Growth

Bonfiglioli's journey in India began in 1999 with the establishment of its first manufacturing plant in Chennai (Thirumudivakkam). This marked the company's strong commitment to the Indian market, quickly becoming a hub for expansion across Asia. Over the years, Bonfiglioli has steadily expanded its operations and reinforced its leadership position in the power transmission sector.

Key milestones in its Indian presence include:

  • 1999: Inauguration of the Chennai plant. 
  • Early 2000s: Continuous growth and expansion, leveraging India as a key market.
  • 2016-2018: Significant investment in expanding existing facilities in Chennai (Thirumudivakkam) and establishing a new facility in Pune. The Chennai expansion included a new 140,000 sq. ft. facility with modern assembly lines, a global R&D center, and test labs.
  • 2024 (and beyond): Further substantial investments, including the foundation stone laying for a new Industry and Automation facility in Cheyyar, Tamil Nadu, and the inauguration of a Technology and Innovation Hub in Chennai. The Cheyyar plant, expected to be operational by 2025, is poised to become one of India's largest gearbox manufacturing facilities.
This continuous investment underscores Bonfiglioli's belief in India's potential and its alignment with the "Make in India" initiative.

Extensive Manufacturing Footprint

Bonfiglioli India boasts a comprehensive manufacturing and assembly footprint designed to serve both domestic and international markets. Its strategically located plants ensure efficient production and timely delivery across various industry segments:

Chennai (Thirumudivakkam): This original and expanded facility is a highly integrated plant focusing on gearboxes and gearmotors primarily for mobile machinery, wind turbines, and industrial processes. It also houses a crucial R&D Centre dedicated to developing tailor-made products for the Indian market.

Pune (Talegaon): This assembly plant caters to a wide range of industries, including food, packaging, cement, steel, pharmaceutical, textile, material handling, sugar, power generation, paper, and water treatment. It significantly boosts production capacity for light and medium-duty gearboxes.

Cheyyar, Tamil Nadu (upcoming): This new, large-scale facility, expected by 2025, will focus on heavy-duty industrial gearboxes, further solidifying Bonfiglioli's position in this segment.

These facilities, combined with a robust after-sales and sales network across India, enable Bonfiglioli to provide localized support and solutions, drastically reducing lead times for customers.
 

Bonfiglioli Technology Space: Global Capability Centre

In the new era of growth, Bonfiglioli group continues its commitment to innovation, excellence & sustainability and with immense gratitude, we inaugurated our innovation hub at Chennai, “BONFIGLIOLI TECHNOLOGY SPACE”.

A state-of-the-art facility will house over 180 engineers with diverse expertise. The center was established to create breakthrough solutions by integrating the most advanced mechanical, electrical, electronic, and hydraulic technologies. It features dedicated testing laboratories to perform accelerated tests that simulate real working conditions, ensuring the highest standards of reliability. As the epicenter of groundbreaking advancements, the Bonfiglioli Technology Space will drive the future of cutting-edge software, advanced electronics, digital innovations, and IoT technologies. This hub will not only enhance Bonfiglioli’s technological capabilities but also solidify its position as a market leader in the industry. A dedicated team will focus on digital technologies, working closely with our R&D and manufacturing plants to enhance production efficiency, by leveraging advanced software solutions, including MES and cybersecurity. This team will provide vital support to all our global manufacturing plants, establishing this center as a true Technology Hub.

Bonfiglioli Technology Space will work closely with our 20 manufacturing plants and over 80 branches worldwide. They will collaborate with other R&D centers and, most importantly, with customers in India and across the globe.


The Technology Space is designed to encourage interaction and creativity. It features collaborative workspaces, a well-stocked library, state-of-the-art conference halls equipped with the latest acoustics and interactive displays and breakout spaces.

With this new Technology Space, Bonfiglioli reaffirms its dedication to driving progress and delivering innovative solutions that keep the world in motion.

Sales Network

Bonfiglioli India has established a strong and widespread sales network to efficiently serve customers across the nation. With 18 strategically located offices in key industrial and commercial hubs, we ensure quick response times, personalized support, and seamless communication with our clients.

Our regional presence enables us to better understand local market needs and deliver solutions that are both effective and timely. Each office is staffed with experienced professionals who are committed to providing top-tier customer service, technical assistance, and after-sales support.

This extensive network reflects our commitment to being close to our customers, enhancing accessibility, and strengthening long-term partnerships across various industries.
 

Service Network

Wherever you are in India, and whatever support your Bonfiglioli product requires, our extensive service network is ready to assist you—swiftly and effectively. Our reach is as wide as our product range, ensuring that no customer is ever too far from expert help.

With a team of highly qualified engineers just a phone call away, and a strong network of authorized Bonfiglioli service partners and franchisees, we are committed to delivering timely, reliable service across the country.

Whether it’s a routine inspection, preventive maintenance, or an urgent repair, our team ensures that your equipment gets the attention it needs—usually within 48 hours. Our goal is to minimize downtime, optimize performance, and give you complete peace of mind.

At Bonfiglioli India, we don’t just deliver products—we build lasting relationships by staying with you every step of the way.
 

Distribution Network

We operate a comprehensive and well-integrated distribution network that ensures seamless availability of our products and solutions across the country. Our network is designed to bring us closer to our customers, enabling faster deliveries, localized support, and responsive service.

With a strong presence of authorized distributors, channel partners, and logistics hubs, we cover key industrial regions across India. This strategic footprint allows us to meet the diverse needs of our customers in sectors such as industrial automation, mobile machinery, and renewable energy.

Every distribution partner is carefully selected and trained to uphold Bonfiglioli’s standards of quality, reliability, and customer service—ensuring that wherever you are, you have access to the same excellence that defines our brand globally.
