Mecwin Technologies

Mecwin Technologies, a leading Indian manufacturer, specializes in advanced solar pumps, MPPT controllers, PMSM motors, fluid pumps, EV motors, and special-purpose motors for medical, defense, and aerospace applications. With a commitment to sustainability and innovation, Mecwin delivers reliable solutions across renewable energy, agriculture, automation, and industrial sectors, driving efficiency and environmental responsibility.

A Journey of Innovation and Growth

Mecwin's journey began in 2017 with a modest 80 sq. ft. facility and a vision to innovate for a greener world. Over the years, the company has expanded its manufacturing capabilities, growing its footprint and workforce to meet the demands of diverse industries. Mecwin’s dedication to technological advancement and sustainability has positioned it as a key player in India’s industrial landscape.

Key Milestones

  • 2017: Founded with 3 employees and an 80 sq. ft. facility in Bengaluru.
  • 2018: Expanded to 8 employees and 400 sq. ft. manufacturing space.
  • 2019: Grew to 25 employees and 3,800 sq. ft. facility, focusing on solar pumps and controllers.
  • 2020: Scaled to 105 employees and 38,000 sq. ft., introducing IoT-enabled products.
  • 2021: Reached 87 employees and 24,000 sq. ft., enhancing EV motor production.
  • 2022: Expanded to 50 employees and 12,000 sq. ft., strengthening automation solutions.
  • 2023: Grew to 145 employees and 50,000 sq. ft., with a focus on advanced motor technologies.
  • 2024: Reached 220 employees and 64,000 sq. ft., expanding renewable energy offerings.
  • 2025: Projected to have 250 employees and 73,000 sq. ft., with plans for a new facility in Pune.
Mecwin’s growth aligns with India’s “Make in India” initiative, emphasizing local manufacturing and sustainable innovation.

Mecwin Innovation Hub

Mecwin Technologies is committed to innovation and sustainability, with the upcoming Mecwin Innovation Hub in Bengaluru. This state-of-the-art facility will house over 100 engineers with expertise in mechanical, electrical, and IoT technologies. The hub will focus on developing advanced solar pumps, EV motors, and IoT solutions, featuring dedicated testing labs to ensure reliability under real-world conditions.

The Innovation Hub will drive advancements in digital technologies, including remote monitoring systems and predictive maintenance, collaborating closely with Mecwin’s manufacturing plants and global partners. It will feature collaborative workspaces, a technical library, and modern conference facilities to foster creativity and innovation.

This hub underscores Mecwin’s dedication to delivering cutting-edge, sustainable solutions for a greener future.


The Mecwin Innovation Hub is designed to promote collaboration and innovation, equipped with advanced testing facilities and interactive workspaces. It will support Mecwin’s mission to lead in sustainable technology and enhance production efficiency through IoT and digital solutions.

By fostering partnerships with customers and R&D centers, the hub will drive Mecwin’s growth as a leader in renewable energy and automation.

Sales Network

Mecwin Technologies has built a robust sales network across India, with strategically located offices in key industrial hubs. This ensures quick response times, personalized support, and seamless communication with customers in renewable energy, agriculture, and automation sectors.

Our regional presence allows us to understand local market needs and deliver tailored solutions efficiently. Each office is staffed with experienced professionals dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and technical support.


 

Service Network

Mecwin’s extensive service network ensures prompt and reliable support for all our products across India. Our team of skilled engineers and authorized service partners is always ready to assist, providing routine maintenance, inspections, or urgent repairs within 48 hours.

We are committed to minimizing downtime and optimizing product performance, ensuring customer satisfaction and long-term reliability. Mecwin’s service network reflects our dedication to building lasting partnerships.


 

Distribution Network

Mecwin’s integrated distribution network ensures seamless product availability across India. With authorized distributors and logistics hubs in key regions, we provide fast deliveries and localized support for solar pumps, EV motors, and IoT solutions.

Our carefully selected partners are trained to uphold Mecwin’s standards of quality and customer service, ensuring that customers receive the same excellence that defines our brand, no matter where they are.
