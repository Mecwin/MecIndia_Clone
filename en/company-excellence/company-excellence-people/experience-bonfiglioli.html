    Our projects

    Evolution and innovation are in our make-up. At Bonfiglioli, you will have the chance to work in a multicultural and multidisciplinary team, taking part in new challenges every day with a disruptive and innovative approach in order to come up with new and smart solutions to problems both big and small. Here are some of the latest projects we have worked on which might interest you!

    E-ngenious

    The E-ngenious platform is part of the digitization process for our Human Capital department, with the goal of creating a new Global Employee Experience. E-ngenious is a digital platform which allows all colleagues around the world to stay connected, empowering the entire company workforce by facilitating bottom-up cooperation.

    Agile

    Bonfiglioli has used an agile design methodology for some years now, through the creation of interdepartmental teams who work together to develop a Minimum Viable Product to respond in a faster, more flexible and more innovative manner to the requirements of customers and internal stakeholders.

    IoT

    We offer customized and ready-to-use IoT solutions to optimize the productivity and efficiency of our customers’ machinery. From design of the applications to monitoring of device health and data collection. The R&D team provides support to customers to allow them to add value to their data and accelerate development.

  • Our site

    Thanks to an international network of closely interconnected production sites and branch offices, we are able to offer the same high levels of quality at any time, anywhere in the world. Our organization is composed of 20 sales offices, thirteen production sites, and over 550 distributors around the world. Our plant and office buildings are designed to the highest standards of safety and technology in order to inspire and promote cooperative working.     Joining Bonfiglioli means making the choice to become part of an organization where the main drivers are human capital, excellence, teamwork and diversity.

    • Onboarding

      Every new adventure brings with it different complexities, such as when joining a new organization. It is important that new hires feel someone is accompanying them on and guiding them in their new adventure.

      For this reason we dedicate significant time and energy to the insertion of new hires within our organization, with the goal of telling them about our organization and values in order to facilitate their entry into the team.

      We organize personalized inductions and days dedicated entirely to newcomers where they can get to know their new colleagues, talk about their experiences, and get to grips with the company in a fun way. We also use virtual tools to help new hires orient themselves within their new context, allowing them to get to know the organization and find their feet within the company.

    • At Bonfiglioli, you will have the chance to express yourself

      Bonfiglioli Academy

      "At Bonfiglioli, we believe that the continuous development of skills and professionalism is the key to being able to tackle the great technological changes and challenges of the future. This is why we set up the Bonfiglioli Academy, our lifelong learning system that brings together the organization’s wealth of know-how and makes it available to everyone, while introducing innovative stimuli to keep up-to-date. The Bonfiglioli Academy promotes self-development, placing each person in charge of their own personal and professional growth path. In fact, all our employees not only have access to dedicated role-specific training courses, but also have the opportunity to choose from a large number of courses based on their professional needs and interests. In the last 4 years we have delivered more than 180,000 hours of training worldwide and have established partnerships with important Schools and Partners"
