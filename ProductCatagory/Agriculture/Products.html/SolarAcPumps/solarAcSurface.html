    Solar AC - Surface Pump

    A high-performance water pumping solution powered by solar energy, featuring durable BLDC/PMSM motors, stainless steel construction, and advanced components for long life, high efficiency, and reliable operation even in tough conditions.

    Key features

    • Extremely efficient BLDC/PMSM motors.
    • Rare Earth magnets which can withstand temperature upto 160°C .
    • High quality segmented carbon thrust bearing.
    • Leak proof motor filled with water for friction less operation.
    • Laser welded impellers.
    • Strainer for sandy water.
    • 100% Stainless steel AISI304 parts

    AC Pump Specifications

    Pump Type
    AC Surface Pump
    Power Range
    1 HP – 5 HP
    Head Range
    10 m – 45 m
    Discharge Range
    228 LPM – 1218 LPM
    Outlet Size
    2" – 4"
    PV Array Capacity
    900 Wp – 4800 Wp

    Note: Maximum Suction in Monoblock Pump set is 7 meter from the seal level

    Applications

    Solar-Powered Irrigation for Sustainable Farming

    This solar AC pump efficiently draws groundwater and delivers it to agricultural fields, reducing dependency on grid electricity or diesel. It enables farmers to irrigate crops cost-effectively while promoting clean energy use.

    Solar AC Pump for Continuous Water Supply

    Equipped with solar panels, this AC pump provides a reliable water source for farms and rural areas. It ensures uninterrupted water flow during sunny hours, helping maintain healthy crops and improve agricultural productivity.
