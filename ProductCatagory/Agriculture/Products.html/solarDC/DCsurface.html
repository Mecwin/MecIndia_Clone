          DC Surface Pump

          The Solar DC surface pump is engineered for efficient water transfer from nearby sources such as lakes, ponds, canals, or rivers. Powered directly by DC sources like solar panels, it delivers consistent performance without dependence on the grid. Designed with robust stainless steel construction, it ensures long operational life even under challenging conditions. The pump range covers 1 HP to 5 HP capacities with dynamic heads from 10 m to 45 m and discharge rates up to 1,354 LPM, making it suitable for irrigation and potable water supply. Advanced BLDC/PMSM motor technology ensures high efficiency, while features like a leak-proof design, high-grade thrust bearings, and sand handling capability enhance reliability in demanding applications.

          Key features

          • Extremely efficient BLDC/PMSM motors.
          • High-grade IPM motors
          • Rare Earth magnets capable of withstanding temperatures up to 160°C.
          • High-quality segmented carbon thrust bearing.
          • Leak-proof motor for smooth and efficient operation.
          • Laser welded impellers for long-lasting performance.
          • Designed for easy maintenance and installation.
          • 100% Stainless steel AISI304 parts
