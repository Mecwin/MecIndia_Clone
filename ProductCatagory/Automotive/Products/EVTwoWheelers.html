          MECWIN Electric Two Wheeler Motors

          MECWIN Electric Two Wheeler Motors are hub motors designed for efficient and sustainable electric mobility. Available in various sizes and power ratings, these motors provide reliable performance for two-wheelers, ensuring optimal speed, torque, and durability in diverse operating conditions.

          Key Features

          • High-performance hub motors designed and made in India
          • Available in 10-inch, 12-inch, 14-inch, 16-inch, and 17-inch models
          • Voltage ratings of 48V/60V/72V for versatile applications
          • Rated power ranging from 550W to 3000W
          • Max speeds from 25Kmph to 100Kmph
          • Brake systems including 110 Drum/Disc and 130 Drum/Disc
          • IP67 rating for water and dust resistance
          • Compatible with hall-based controllers supporting CAN/NON CAN
