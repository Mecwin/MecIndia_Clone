          MECWIN Electric Three Wheeler Motors

          MECWIN Electric Three Wheeler Motors are mid-drive motors designed for robust and efficient performance in electric three-wheelers. These motors deliver high power and speed, ensuring reliable operation in demanding conditions, with advanced cooling and gear systems for enhanced durability and efficiency.

          Key Features

          • High-performance mid-drive motors designed and made in India
          • Voltage ratings of 48V/60V/72V for versatile applications
          • Rated power ranging from 1000W to 5000W
          • Max speeds from 2000 RPM to 5000 RPM
          • Forced air or natural cooling systems
          • Helical or spline gear types for efficient power transfer
          • IP67 rating for water and dust resistance
          • Compatible with hall-based and encoder-based controllers supporting CAN/NON CAN
