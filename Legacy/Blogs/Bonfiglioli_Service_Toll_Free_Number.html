Keeping Your Drive Systems Running Smoothly 

Protect Your Productivity with Bonfiglioli Service.
In the world of industrial automation, unplanned downtime is more than a nuisance—it’s a business risk. Machine failures can lead to delayed shipments, missed deadlines, and rising maintenance expenses. Every minute lost impacts your bottom line.

That’s where Bonfiglioli Service comes in. With our comprehensive support structure—featuring a dedicated Toll-Free Number and expertly managed Annual Maintenance Contracts—we empower you to stay ahead of breakdowns and extend equipment life.

Whether it’s real-time support, periodic inspections, or predictive maintenance planning, Bonfiglioli ensures that your gearboxes and gearmotors remain in optimal working condition, all year round.

📞 Immediate Support, Wherever You Are 

Bonfiglioli’s dedicated toll-free service number connects you directly with expert service engineers—available for: 

  • Troubleshooting and diagnostics 
  • Emergency repair guidance 
  • Technical consultation and AMC coordination 
AMC Plans Built for Industrial Needs 

Bonfiglioli offers two flexible AMC packages tailored to the specific needs of modern manufacturing and automation facilities: 

🥇 Gold AMC Plan 

For businesses that prioritize maximum uptime: 

  • Scheduled visits (quarterly or half-yearly) 
  • Two additional emergency breakdown visits 
  • Preventive + responsive maintenance 
  • Guaranteed system reliability and reduced unplanned stoppages 

🥈 Silver AMC Plan 

A flexible and cost-effective solution: 

  • On-call service, billed per visit 
  • Expert visit within 48 hours 
  • Fixed service charges for 12 months 
  • Quick spare part support 




🔄 Reconditioning & Repair Services 

  • Available for reconditioning which extends the life of the gearbox
  • Universal repair centre at our headquarters follows the best practices
  • Works with highly qualiﬁed technicians
  • Uses high standard benchmarks for repairs and reconditioning of the gearboxed
