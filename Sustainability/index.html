        "Sustainability isn't just about the future, but about the choices we make today."

        This is the vision upheld by our CEO Sonia Bonfiglioli and the guiding force behind all areas of our company strategy.

        Our commitment to developing a sustainable business model led to the creation of our Sustainability Policy: an authentic company manifesto that outlines a clear set of carefully defined objectives in line with the ESG pillars: Environmental, Social and Governance.

        Ambitious targets without a concrete plan risk remaining mere desires and dreams. That's why we compiled our Sustainability Roadmap: an action plan that will guide us, step by step, towards the sustainable and fair future that we hope to build between now and 2030.

        Focus on: regulatory compliance

        To ensure we can accurately measure the Group's impact on the environment, we comply with various international industry frameworks, including the Global Reporting Initiative (GRI), the Sustainability Accounting Standards Board (SASB), the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and the Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM). By conforming to these parameters, we are able to identify and quantify the impacts our activities have on the environment, people and society in a way that is clear, uniform and comparable. These parameters are also an integral part of the Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD), which requires companies to report on their environmental footprint. The standards we have adopted set universal sustainability targets that all companies and countries must strive towards in order to reduce their impact on the planet.

        Sustainability: what we've achieved

        We have the tools to accurately measure the results of our work, and periodically record them in the form of clear, transparent reports.
